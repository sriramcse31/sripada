Temple Legacy

The Sacred History of Pithapuram

Pithapuram (formerly Pithikapuram) is one of the most sacred pilgrimage centres in Andhra Pradesh, revered as the birthplace of Lord Sripada Srivallabha, the first complete incarnation of Lord Dattatreya in Kaliyuga.

The Mahasamsthanam stands as a beacon of spiritual solace, drawing devotees from across the globe who seek the divine blessings of the Lord. The temple architecture, though steeped in ancient tradition, has been thoughtfully preserved and modernised to accommodate the growing number of pilgrims while maintaining its inherent sanctity and peaceful atmosphere.

"To visit Pithapuram is to walk on the very ground where the Lord performed his divine leelas. It is a place of profound spiritual awakening and absolute purity."

The temple complex houses the Swayambhu Padukas (divine footprints) of Lord Sripada Srivallabha, which are the focal point of daily worship. The administration, under the AP Endowments Department, ensures transparent operations, regular Nithya Annadanam (free feeding), and impeccable maintenance of the sacred premises.
