Getting Here

Pithapuram has its own railway station — Pithapuram Railway Station (PTM) — on the Howrah–Chennai main line (East Coast Railway). Trains from Vijayawada, Visakhapatnam, Chennai, and Kolkata all pass through or connect here via Samalkot / Kakinada junction.

Tip: If your train does not stop at Pithapuram (PTM), alight at Samalkot Junction (SLO) (~12 km away) or Kakinada Town (CCT) and take a bus or auto-rickshaw to Pithapuram. Check current schedules on IRCTC.

From Station to Temple

electric_rickshaw

Auto-Rickshaw

Available outside the station. ~₹30–50, 5 min ride. Ask for "Sripada Srivallabha temple".

directions_walk

On Foot

~20 min walk from the station. Signboards mark the route through town.

Pithapuram is well-served by APSRTC (Andhra Pradesh State Road Transport Corporation) buses from all major cities in Andhra Pradesh and Telangana. The town has its own bus stand located in the heart of Pithapuram.

Arriving by Air
