Nitya Seva

Annadanam

Food Donation

Annadanam is the sacred act of offering cooked food to all who arrive at the temple. Daily meals are served free of charge to outstation devotees, pilgrims, and the needy who travel long distances to seek the blessings of Lord Sripada Srivallabha. Your donation ensures that no one who comes in devotion leaves hungry.

The scriptures declare "Annadanam Param Danam" — food donation is the greatest of all gifts. By participating in this seva you accumulate punya not only for yourself but for your entire family lineage.
