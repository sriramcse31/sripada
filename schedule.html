Daily Service Programme

  • schedule 5:00 a.m.

    Suprabhat

  • schedule 5:30 a.m.

    Kakad Harathi

  • schedule 5:30–6:00 a.m.

    Abhishek to Original Padukas

    Abhishek to the Original Padukas of Sree Swamy with pure water. Devotees should see this service without fail. Afterwards Dhoop Harathi.

  • schedule 9:00 a.m.

    Panchamrutha Abhishek

    Abhishek with Panchamrutha to the lotus feet of Sree Swami and worship. Afterwards offering of Harathi and recitation of Mantra Pushpa, distribution of Prasadam.

  • schedule 11:30 a.m.

    Dhoop Harathi

  • schedule 12:00 noon

    Maha Naivedya & Harathi

  • schedule 5:00 p.m.

    Pradosha Worship

    Dhoop Harathi, Neerajana, Mantra Pushpa.

  • schedule 7:00 p.m.

    Palanquin Service

    Palanquin service to Sree Swamy.

  • schedule 8:30 p.m.

    Harathi
