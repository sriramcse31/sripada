Abhishekam

Panchamurta Abhisheka Sahita Ashotottra Pooja

schedule 9:00 a.m.

Lord Dattatreya is the embodiment of 'Trimurthis'. Abhishekams with Panchamrutham (Cow Milk, Cow Curd, Cow Ghee, Honey and Sugar) are performed. As the Lord is fond of Abhishekams, Rudra Namaka Chamakam is also recited.

The first incarnation of Dattatreya is Sripada Srivallabha. By performing this Seva, Devotees are blessed with the Lord's Anugraha and their desires and wishes will be fulfilled.

Rudrabhishekam

Mahanyasapoorvaka Ekadasa Rudrabhisha Sahita Ashtottara Pooja

schedule 9:00 a.m.

The Lotus feet of the lord are worshipped with eleven items (Cow Milk, Cow Curd, Cow Ghee, Honey, Sugar, Sandal Wood, Vibhuthi, Perfume, Rosewater, Fruit Juice and Vattiverllu) 11 times along with Rudranamaka chamakams.

Of the 'Thrimurthis', Rudra is fond of Abhishekam. Lord Sripada Srivallabha is the incarnation of Dattatreya — so this Abhishekam is very much liked by him. All Devotees are requested to take part and receive his blessings in plenty.

Datta Mahamantra Homam

Datta Mahamantra Homam to SriPada Srivallabha after Abhishekam

schedule After Abhishekam

If 108 times 'Datta Mahamantra Homam' is performed and the celestial deities are invoked and satisfied by offering 'Havissu', Devotees are protected from problems of 'Gocharam' and their wishes are fulfilled.

The welfare of all takes place through this sacred homam. The Lord's divine protection is extended to all participants and their families.

Pallaki Seva

Pallaki Seva after different Haratis to Sripada Srivallabha

schedule 7:00 p.m.

Devotees carry the Pallaki of Lord Sripada Srivallabha on their shoulders and walk around the Mandir 3 times singing Sankeerthanas and Bhajanas.

The pleased Lord Sripada Srivallabha bestows devotees with name, fame, Health and Prosperity.

Rajita Pallaki Seva

Pallaki Seva with Silver Pallaki after different Haratis

schedule 7:00 p.m.

Laghu Pooja is performed to the Holy Padukas of Guru Dattatreya Sripada Srivallabha on Thursdays, Days of Chitta Star and Special Occasions with a silver Pallaki. Prasadam will be sent by post.

Devotees carry the Rajitha (Silver) Pallaki around the Mandir 3 times singing Sankeerthanas and Bhajanas so the pleased Lord bestows name, fame, Health and Prosperity.

Maha Mruthyumjaya Homam

Every Thursday — Maha Mruthyumjaya Homam will be performed

schedule Every Thursday 10 a.m.

The Maha Mruthyumjaya Homam is a powerful Vedic fire ritual dedicated to Lord Shiva in his aspect as the conqueror of death. Performed every Thursday from 10 AM onwards, it bestows long life, health, and protection.
